Baby Sleeping Sounds


This Android app provides sounds that is similar to mother's womb. These sounds are also called white noises. They calm babies imitating the environment of mother's womb. The app also has some soothing music. So try it on your baby while you're trying to make him/her sleep and choose his/her favourite.

Speak Translate


English-Turkish dictionary app. Search your term either with typing term in both languages (no need to toggle tr-en, en-tr states) or with voice action of chosen language. The app also records your term search history.
